Research date: 13-06-2026.

## Salesforce Integration Depth (Critical Competitive Gap)

**Fireflies (benchmark):** Full integration - auto-creates Leads, Contacts, Opportunities; syncs transcripts, summaries, and action items as Salesforce Tasks; configurable auto-create vs. log-only; manual resync of historical meetings. Available on all paid plans including Pro ($10/seat). Two-year production maturity.

**Otter:** Salesforce and HubSpot sync on Pro ($8.33/seat/month annual). Details on object mapping not publicly disclosed.

**Fathom:** Salesforce and HubSpot on all paid tiers ($16+/seat/month). CRM field sync on Business ($25/seat). 

**Notion AI:** Salesforce in Enterprise Search alpha only. Not a CRM integration in the traditional sense.

**Granola:** CRM push to HubSpot, Attio, Affinity on Business tier ($14/seat). No Salesforce documented on official site.

**MeetFlow gap:** Salesforce in roadmap for May 2026 (Dana Rivera's team). Risk: v1 scope must include Task creation and configurable object mapping to match Fireflies minimum viable depth. A log-only sync is insufficient for sales-team buyers.

## Integration Ecosystem Scale
- Fireflies: 100+ integrations across 12 categories (video conferencing, CRM, project management, note-taking, storage, collaboration, email, calendaring, ATS, dialer, workflow automation)
- MeetFlow: Notion, Slack, Jira, Linear (4 documented, per product.md)
- Granola: 6+ documented (Notion, Slack, HubSpot, Attio, Affinity, Zapier) on Business tier
- Otter: Selective - CRM (Salesforce, HubSpot), Zapier, Make
- Fathom: CRM, Zapier, Make, MCP, public API

## Key Pricing Notes (as of 13-06-2026)
- Fireflies Pro: $10/seat/month (annual) - cheapest paid CRM-sync tier in the category
- Otter Pro: $8.33/seat/month (annual) - cheapest overall with Salesforce sync
- MeetFlow Pro: $15/seat/month - priced above both Fireflies Pro and Otter Pro
