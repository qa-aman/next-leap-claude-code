Research date: 13-06-2026. All data from official pricing pages. Monthly billing unless noted.

## Otter.ai
Source: https://otter.ai/pricing
- Basic: Free - 300 min/mo transcription, 3 lifetime file imports, 20 AI Chat queries/mo, 5 AI templates/user, 5 workspace members max, 25 conversation history
- Pro: $16.99/user/mo (annual: $8.33)
- Business: $30.00/user/mo (annual: $20.00)
- Enterprise: Custom

## Fireflies.ai
Source: https://fireflies.ai/pricing
- Free: 400 min total team storage, unlimited transcription, limited AI summaries, 2-hr max per recording
- Pro: $18/user/mo (annual: $10)
- Business: $29/user/mo (annual: $19)
- Enterprise: $39/user/mo (annual: $39 - no discount)

Note: Free plan storage is per-team, not per-user.

## Granola
Source: https://www.granola.ai/pricing
- Basic (Free): Unlimited meetings, limited meeting history (retention not publicly specified), AI notes + AI Chat + shared folders + custom templates included
- Business: $14/user/mo
- Enterprise: $35/user/mo (SSO, SCIM, governance controls)

Note: Granola free plan is the most permissive in the set - AI notes included at no cost.

## Notion AI
Source: https://www.notion.com/pricing and https://www.notion.com/help/ai-meeting-notes
- Free: $0 - AI trial only, no AI Meeting Notes, 7-day history, 5MB file cap
- Plus: $10/user/mo - AI trial only (AI Meeting Notes NOT included)
- Business: $20/user/mo - Full AI Meeting Notes access (required for meeting features)
- Enterprise: Custom - Full access + zero LLM data retention option

KEY POINT: Notion's "free bundled meeting summaries" framing in competitive.md is outdated or partially wrong.
AI Meeting Notes requires Business plan ($20/user/mo). It is not free for all Notion users.
Free and Plus users only get a trial of AI capabilities. See [[competitor-features-ai-intelligence]].
