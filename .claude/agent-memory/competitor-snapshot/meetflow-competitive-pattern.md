MeetFlow's stated competitive advantage is AI quality + integrations + team features combined. No single competitor matches all three.

**Where MeetFlow consistently wins:** Cross-platform reach, team/multi-seat capability, integration breadth (Slack, Notion, Jira, Linear, Salesforce coming May 2026), and enterprise roadmap (SSO, audit logs, on-prem, June 2026 GA).

**Where MeetFlow consistently loses:** Design and UX trust (Granola), distribution and bundling (Notion AI), CRM-native workflows (Fireflies), brand recognition with free-tier users (Otter.ai).

**The core vulnerability:** MeetFlow's "AI quality" positioning is undermined by 66% action item accuracy. The value prop depends on reliability. At 66% accuracy, power users double-check everything manually, eroding the time-saving claim and opening the door for design-led competitors like Granola to win on trust.

**Consistent strategic signal:** Every competitor analysis converges on action item accuracy as the load-bearing moat. Fix accuracy first, then the roadmap bets (Smart Follow-Up April, Salesforce May, Enterprise June, Transcript Accuracy v2 June) compound on a credible foundation.
