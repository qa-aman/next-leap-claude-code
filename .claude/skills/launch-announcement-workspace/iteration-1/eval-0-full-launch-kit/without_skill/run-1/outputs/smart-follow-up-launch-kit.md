## 1. Launch Announcement Copy

**Headline:**
Smart Follow-Up is here - your meetings now write themselves.

**Sub-headline:**
MeetFlow automatically drafts your follow-up email the moment a meeting ends. Every action item flagged. Nothing missed.

**Body:**

Every meeting ends with the same problem: someone has to write the follow-up. It's manual, it's slow, and it's where action items go to die.

Starting today, MeetFlow does it for you.

Smart Follow-Up generates a complete follow-up email the moment your meeting ends - subject line, summary, and a bulleted action item list. Each action item is scored automatically. The ones that need your attention are flagged before you send. The ones that are ready go straight into the draft.

You review it. You edit what needs editing. You send.

That's it.

**What's included at launch:**
- Auto-drafted follow-up email generated within 2 minutes of meeting end
- Action items labeled Ready or Needs Review - so you know what to check
- Direct export to Slack or Notion alongside email send
- Works on Zoom, Google Meet, and Microsoft Teams

**Plan availability:**
- Free: up to 5 follow-up drafts per month
- Pro: unlimited drafts, confidence scoring, all export channels
- Team: everything in Pro, plus team-wide follow-up analytics (sent rate, edit rate by team)

## 2. Customer Email

**Subject line:**
Your meetings now write their own follow-ups

**Preview text:**
Smart Follow-Up is live - drafts ready in under 2 minutes, action items flagged before you send.

---

Hi [First Name],

One thing keeps coming up in feedback: action items don't survive the meeting.

They get missed in the transcript. They get lost in the follow-up email no one has time to write. By the time the next standup rolls around, half of them have evaporated.

We built Smart Follow-Up to close that loop.

Here's how it works:

1. Your meeting ends.
2. MeetFlow generates a complete follow-up draft within 2 minutes - subject line, summary, action items included.
3. You open the draft. Action items are labeled **Ready** (confident) or **Needs Review** (flagged for you to check).
4. Edit what needs editing. Delete what doesn't belong.
5. Send via email, post to Slack, or export to Notion. Your call.

Nothing sends automatically. Every draft waits for your explicit approval.

For Pro and Team users, this is available today with no setup required. Open any recent meeting and you'll see the "Draft Follow-Up" prompt in your dashboard or Slack digest.

Free plan users get 5 drafts per month starting now.

Questions? Reply to this email or reach out at support@meetflow.com.

- The MeetFlow Team

## 4. Messaging Framework (for Sales and Support reference)

**Core message:**
Smart Follow-Up closes the loop that action items keep falling through. It drafts the email, flags what's uncertain, and waits for your approval.

**For power users (Sarah Chen archetype - 8-10 meetings/day):**
Stop spending 30 minutes per meeting reviewing and rewriting. The draft is ready in 2 minutes. Flagged items tell you exactly where to focus. You send faster, with more confidence.

**For skeptical users (Marcus Okafor archetype - privacy-focused):**
Nothing sends automatically. The review screen shows you exactly what will be sent, where it's going, and who will receive it - before you click Send. Full control, every time.

**For casual users (Priya Nair archetype - Slack-first):**
Access the draft directly from your Slack digest. Review, edit, and post to Slack without opening the MeetFlow dashboard.

**What it is NOT:**
- Not automatic sending (always requires your approval)
- Not a calendar tool (no follow-up call scheduling)
- Not multi-language (English only at launch)
- Jira and Linear export: coming May 2026
