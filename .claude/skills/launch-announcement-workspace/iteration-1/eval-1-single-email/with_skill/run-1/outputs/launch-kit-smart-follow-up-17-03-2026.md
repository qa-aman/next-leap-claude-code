**Date:** 17-03-2026 | **Launch target:** April 2026

## Positioning Summary

- **Alternatives:** Re-reading transcripts and writing follow-ups by hand; Otter (transcription, no confidence scoring); Notion AI (bundled, no action item reliability) (source: `03-product-knowledge/competitive.md`)
- **Unique capabilities:** Confidence-scored action items (Ready / Needs review) surfaced before anything sends; review screen where you edit, delete, or approve; Slack and Notion export at launch (source: `08-product-features/01-smart-follow-up/prd-smart-follow-ups.md`)
- **Value:** Know which action items to trust without re-reading the transcript; cut meeting follow-up from 30 minutes of manual correction to under 2 minutes (source: PRD review screen design section, `05-user-personas/sarah-chen.md`)
- **Target customer:** Pro users in the Sarah Chen segment - high-volume meeting schedulers who use MeetFlow action items as their task system and are losing trust due to the 34% error rate (source: `03-product-knowledge/company.md`, `05-user-personas/sarah-chen.md`)
- **Category frame:** Follow-up automation - not transcription, not note-taking (source: `08-product-features/01-smart-follow-up/prd-smart-follow-ups.md`)

## 2. Customer Email

**Subject:** Your follow-up email, ready before your next meeting starts

---

Right now, 34% of the action items MeetFlow extracts are wrong or missing. You know this, because you spend time re-checking them before you send anything important.

Smart Follow-Up changes that. After your meeting ends, MeetFlow drafts a follow-up email with every action item already labeled - **Ready** (confident) or **Needs review** (check this one). You see the full draft before anything leaves your inbox. Edit, remove, approve - then send in under 2 minutes.

No auto-sending. No surprises. You stay in control.

Smart Follow-Up is coming to Pro in April 2026. It works with Slack and Notion from day one.
