# Smart Summaries v2.4 - Social Post

**Platform:** LinkedIn
**Status:** Draft
**Date drafted:** 06-06-2026

---

Most AI meeting tools give you more. More words. More paragraphs. More scrolling.

We went the other way.

Smart Summaries v2.4 shipped in November 2025 and we never actually talked about what changed.

The old model averaged 350 words to summarise a 30-minute meeting. Useful, but not something you'd read on your way to the next one.

Three things we fixed:

- Summaries are now 30% shorter on average
- Key decisions surface at the top, not buried in paragraph three
- Consistent structure every time: decisions first, discussion points second, context last

The signal that told us it worked: "summaries too long" was the #2 NPS complaint before this release, with 14 mentions in our Q1 2026 survey. After v2.4, it dropped off the list entirely. Promoters now cite summaries as a reason they recommend MeetFlow.

NPS went from 22 to 34 in that same period.

Less really is more. Your next meeting summary proves it.
