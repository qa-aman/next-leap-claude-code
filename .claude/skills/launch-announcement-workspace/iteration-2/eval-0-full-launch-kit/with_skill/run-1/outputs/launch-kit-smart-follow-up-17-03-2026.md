# Launch Kit - Smart Follow-Up

**Date:** 17-03-2026 | **Launch target:** April 2026

---

## Positioning Summary

- **Alternatives:** Re-reading transcripts and writing follow-up emails manually; or skipping the follow-up entirely and losing accountability. (source: `08-product-features/01-smart-follow-up/prd-smart-follow-ups.md`, `03-product-knowledge/competitive.md`)
- **Unique capabilities:** (1) Confidence-scored action items - each item labeled Ready (>=70% confidence) or Needs review (<70%) before the draft is sent. (2) One-screen review flow designed to take under 2 minutes, not 30. (3) Direct send or export to Slack and Notion from the same screen. (source: `08-product-features/01-smart-follow-up/prd-smart-follow-ups.md`)
- **Value:** Reliable confidence labeling means users stop re-checking every action item manually. The review-and-send workflow closes the follow-up loop in under 2 minutes versus the current 30-minute manual correction cycle. Accuracy target: 80% by end of Q2 2026, up from 66% today. (source: `prd-smart-follow-ups.md`, `03-product-knowledge/company.md`)
- **Target customer:** Primary - Sarah Chen (Head of Product, Pro user, 8-10 meetings/day). She spends 30 minutes per meeting in manual correction and cannot stop double-checking because action item accuracy is 66%. She is blocked from upgrading a team of 8 to Team plan ($4,700/yr) by accuracy alone. (source: `05-user-personas/sarah-chen.md`, `prd-smart-follow-ups.md`)
- **Category frame:** Follow-up automation within AI meeting assistant - specifically the handoff from "meeting recorded" to "follow-up sent". (source: `03-product-knowledge/company.md`, `04-strategy/product-vision.md`)

## 1. Announcement Blurb

You finish a meeting and face 20 minutes of cleanup: re-read the transcript, figure out which action items actually matter, draft the follow-up, send it. Every time. And even after all that, 1 in 3 action items is still wrong.

Smart Follow-Up closes the loop. After your meeting ends, MeetFlow drafts the follow-up email automatically - subject line, summary, and each action item labeled Ready or Needs review based on confidence scoring. You open one screen, check the flagged items, and send. The whole review takes under 2 minutes.

No more 30-minute correction cycles. No more wondering what got dropped.

Smart Follow-Up ships in April 2026 for Pro and Team users. Free users get up to 5 drafts per month, without confidence scores.

Turn it on in your MeetFlow dashboard when it goes live.
