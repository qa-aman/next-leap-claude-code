## Positioning Summary

- **Alternatives:** Re-reading transcript and writing follow-ups manually (~30 min/meeting); manually correcting MeetFlow action items item by item. (source: `05-user-personas/sarah-chen.md`, `08-product-features/01-smart-follow-up/prd-smart-follow-ups.md`)

- **Target customer:** Pro users matching Sarah Chen - high meeting volume, action items as primary task system, upgrade blocked by accuracy distrust. (source: `05-user-personas/sarah-chen.md`)
- **Category frame:** Follow-up automation within AI meeting assistant. (source: `03-product-knowledge/company.md`, `prd-smart-follow-ups.md`)

## Customer Email

**Subject:** Your follow-up still takes 30 minutes. It shouldn't.

---

You finish a meeting and sit down to write the follow-up. First you re-read the transcript. Then you sort through the action items and fix the ones that are wrong - which is nearly 1 in 3. Then you send. That's 30 minutes of work MeetFlow should be doing for you.

Smart Follow-Up changes this. After each meeting, you get a drafted follow-up email ready to review. Every action item is labeled Ready or Needs review, so you know exactly where to focus. You check the flagged items, edit if needed, and send - directly to email, Slack, or Notion. Target review time: under 2 minutes.

Smart Follow-Up is available now on your Pro plan. Open your next meeting summary to try it.
