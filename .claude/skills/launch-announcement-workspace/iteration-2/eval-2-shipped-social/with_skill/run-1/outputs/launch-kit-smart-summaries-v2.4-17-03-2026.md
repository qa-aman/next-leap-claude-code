# Launch Kit - Smart Summaries v2.4
**Date:** 17-03-2026 | **Launch target:** GA (shipped November 2025)

## Positioning Summary
- **Alternatives:** Manually re-reading long summaries or using tools like Otter/Notion AI that produce generic transcript highlights rather than structured synthesis (source: competitive.md)
- **Unique capabilities:** Summaries 30% shorter on average; key decisions surfaced at the top; consistent structure - decisions first, then discussion, then context (source: release-v2.4-smart-summaries.md)
- **Value:** Users can identify the 3 decisions that matter without reading an essay - critical information is immediately visible, not buried (source: release-v2.4-smart-summaries.md, feedback-q1-2026.md via release notes)
- **Target customer:** Sarah Chen (Pro, 8-10 meetings/day, no buffer time between calls) - primary. She uses summaries as a daily record and needs to move fast (source: sarah-chen.md)
- **Category frame:** AI meeting assistant - specifically the "meeting summary" layer of that category (source: company.md)
