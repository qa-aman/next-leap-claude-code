## 13. Other Important Reads

Curated reads on where PM and tech careers are headed in the AI era. Skim the summary, then open the link if it lands.

- **"Your Job Is Changing Faster Than You Think"** by Elena Verna — https://www.elenaverna.com/p/youll-lose-your-job-in-2027
  *A blunt warning that most tech roles will look very different by 2027. Verna lays out three moves: honestly audit whether you enjoy your job, build career optionality (solopreneurship, indie SaaS), and climb the AI-native skill ladder from "AI as writing buddy" up to "shipping production agents." Read it for a practical framework to future-proof your career.*

- **"Why Half of Product Managers Are in Trouble"** by Nikhyl Singhal (on Lenny's Podcast) — https://www.lennysnewsletter.com/p/why-half-of-product-managers-are-in-trouble
  *Nikhyl Singhal (ex-Meta, ex-Google, founder of The Skip) argues half of today's PMs will be displaced in the next two years unless they reinvent around AI. The core message: brand-name resumes and "smiling exhaustion" won't save you - PMs who genuinely engage with AI tools and become AI-native operators will thrive while orgs restructure around them. Essential listen for any PM serious about staying relevant.*

- **Lenny's Newsletter** by Lenny Rachitsky — https://www.lennysnewsletter.com/
  *The most-read publication for product, growth, and career advice (1.2M+ subscribers). Deeply researched essays and interviews with operators from the best product companies. Subscribe for a weekly source of frameworks, benchmarks, and tactical playbooks for PMs and founders.*
