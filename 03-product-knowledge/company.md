# MeetFlow — Company Overview

## What We Do

MeetFlow is an AI-powered meeting assistant that records, transcribes, and summarizes meetings across Zoom, Google Meet, and Microsoft Teams. It automatically extracts action items, generates shareable summaries, and integrates with the tools teams already use.

The product sits at the intersection of productivity and AI. Meetings are where decisions happen, but 90% of what's discussed gets lost within 24 hours. MeetFlow captures everything and turns conversations into structured, actionable outputs.

## Current Priorities

1. **Retention improvement** — Free-to-Pro conversion is 6.2%, but Pro churn hit 4.1% monthly in Q4 2025. Action item accuracy is the top complaint driving churn.
2. **Enterprise tier launch (Q2 2026)** — SSO, admin controls, audit logs, on-prem transcript storage option. Three enterprise pilots in progress.
3. **Salesforce integration** — Most-requested integration from sales-heavy teams. Direct competitive pressure from Fireflies.

## Key Risks

- **Notion AI** is bundling meeting summary features free for existing Notion users. This directly threatens MeetFlow's free tier and casual users.
- **Granola** is gaining fast among prosumer power users with a Mac-native, beautifully designed experience.
- **Action item accuracy** at 66% (34% wrong or missing) is eroding trust with the most valuable user segment — power users who rely on action items as their task system.
