# MeetFlow - Q2 2026 OKRs

> OKRs = Objectives and Key Results. An Objective is a qualitative goal (the "what"). Key Results are measurable outcomes (the "how you know you got there").
> For vision context, see `04-strategy/product-vision.md`. For current feature status, see `03-product-knowledge/product.md`.

---

## Objective 1: Make AI output reliable enough to trust

Users abandon features when the AI gets things wrong. At 66% action item accuracy, we're not there yet.

| Key Result | Target | Current |
|---|---|---|
| Action item accuracy (correct and complete) | 80% | 66% |
| Transcription accuracy in standard environments | 96% | 92% |
| Smart Follow-Up adoption rate (% of Pro users who send at least one follow-up) | 40% | 0% (ships April) |

---

## Objective 2: Grow Team plan adoption

Team is our highest-revenue tier at $49/seat/month. We have 200 seats today. Enterprise GA in June opens the door to larger buyers.

| Key Result | Target | Current |
|---|---|---|
| Team plan seats | 400 | 200 |
| Free-to-Pro conversion rate | 8% | 6.2% |
| Meeting Pattern Insights weekly digest open rate | 50% | 43% |

---

## Objective 3: Stop Pro users from leaving

4.1% monthly Pro churn compounds fast. Reducing it is the highest-ROI lever on ARR (Annual Recurring Revenue - total yearly subscription revenue).

| Key Result | Target | Current |
|---|---|---|
| Pro monthly churn rate | 2.5% | 4.1% |
| NPS (Net Promoter Score - how likely users are to recommend MeetFlow) | 45 | 34 |
| Pro users with Salesforce Integration active (ships May) | 500 | 0 |

---

## How to Use This

- Review these weekly in your team standup.
- Flag any Key Result trending wrong before the end of the quarter.
- Don't add new Key Results mid-quarter - scope lock is intentional.
