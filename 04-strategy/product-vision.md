# MeetFlow - Product Vision

> For full company context, see `03-product-knowledge/company.md`.

---

## Vision Statement

MeetFlow becomes the AI layer every meeting-heavy team depends on.
Not just transcription - the system that turns meetings into decisions and action.
Your meeting data works for you, not against you.

---

## Who It's For

- **Free users (12,000):** Individuals who want meeting notes without manual effort.
- **Pro users (2,800):** Professionals who need reliable action tracking and follow-up.
- **Team users (200):** Managers and leads who need visibility across multiple meetings.

---

| ARR | $3M | Up |

---

## Current Gaps to Close

- **Action items are wrong 34% of the time.** Users can't trust what MeetFlow captures.
- **Pro churn is 4.1% monthly.** Too many users don't see enough value to stay.
- **Only 200 Team seats.** Enterprise motion hasn't started yet.
- **Transcription drops in noisy environments.** 92% is quiet-room only.
- **Conversion at 6.2%.** Most Free users never upgrade.

---

## What We're Building Toward

- Smart Follow-Up ships in April 2026. It closes the action item loop.
- Salesforce Integration ships in May. It connects meetings to revenue data.
- Enterprise Tier goes GA in June. It opens a new buyer segment.
- Transcript Accuracy v2 ships in June. It fixes reliability outside quiet rooms.

These four bets address the gaps above directly.
