# Sarah Chen - "The Power User"

## Quick Snapshot

- **Role:** Head of Product
- **Age:** 34
- **Company:** Series C startup, 120 employees
- **Plan:** Pro ($15/mo), solo account

## A Typical Day

- You run 8-10 meetings daily, most back-to-back.
- You have no buffer time between calls.
- By end of day, you have no energy left for manual note-writing.
- MeetFlow recordings are your safety net across every meeting.

## How You Use MeetFlow

- Record every single meeting - no exceptions.
- Use AI-generated action items as your primary task list.
- Push summaries directly to Notion after each session.
- Review action items at end of day as a final check.

## What You Love

> "MeetFlow is the only reason I survive my calendar. Before this, I was spending 45 minutes every evening writing up notes from the day."

- The time savings are real and daily.
- Notion integration fits your existing workflow without friction.
- Having a record of every meeting removes anxiety about missing details.

## What Frustrates You

- Action item accuracy is the core problem.
- Confidence scores say "high" but critical items still get missed.
- You double-check every action item manually - defeating the purpose.
- Trust in the AI is eroding the more this happens.

## What Would Make You Happy

- Confidence scores that are actually reliable.
- Fewer false "high confidence" labels on incomplete items.
- Enough trust to stop manually reviewing every output.

## Revenue Potential

- Currently on Pro: $15/mo ($180/yr).
- Ready to move a team of 8 to Team plan: $49/seat = $4,700/yr.
- Upgrade is blocked entirely by accuracy, not price.

## Churn Risk

**Low** - too embedded to leave easily. But trust is eroding. If accuracy doesn't improve, she stops advocating internally and the team upgrade never happens.
