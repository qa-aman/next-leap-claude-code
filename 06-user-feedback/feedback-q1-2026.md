# Q1 2026 NPS Survey — Aggregated Results

**Survey period:** January 5-19, 2026
**Respondents:** 47 (Pro and Team plan users only)
**Method:** In-app survey + email follow-up

---

## NPS Score: 34

| Category | Count | Percentage |
|----------|-------|-----------|
| Promoters (9-10) | 19 | 40% |
| Passives (7-8) | 14 | 30% |
| Detractors (0-6) | 14 | 30% |

Previous NPS: 22 (Q3 2025). The 12-point improvement tracks with the Smart Summaries v2 launch in November 2025, which shortened average summary length by 30%.

---

## Top Complaints (open-ended responses, coded by theme)

| Theme | Mentions | Example verbatim |
|-------|----------|-----------------|
| Action items wrong or missing | 19 | "I can't trust the action items. I still have to go back and check every single one." |
| Summaries too long | 14 | "I want bullet points, not an essay. Give me the 3 things that matter." |
| Need Salesforce integration | 11 | "We're a sales org. No Salesforce = no deal for Team plan." |
| Privacy/data concerns | 8 | "Where are recordings stored? Can I auto-delete after 30 days?" |
| Too expensive for light use | 7 | "I use it twice a week. $15/month doesn't feel worth it." |

---

## Top Praise (open-ended responses, coded by theme)

| Theme | Mentions | Example verbatim |
|-------|----------|-----------------|
| Saves 20-30 min per day | 16 | "I get back 30 minutes every day. That alone is worth the subscription." |
| Slack digest is great | 12 | "The Slack integration is the killer feature. My whole team reads the digests." |
| Best meeting tool I've used | 9 | "Tried Otter, tried Fireflies. MeetFlow is the best overall package." |

---

## Key Finding

The gap between promoters and detractors is explained almost entirely by action item accuracy. Promoters tend to use MeetFlow for summaries and Slack digests (features that work well). Detractors rely on action items as their task management system and get burned by the 34% error rate.

**Implication:** Fixing action item accuracy doesn't just improve NPS — it converts detractors into promoters because the underlying product value (time savings) is already proven. The AI quality is the bottleneck, not the core product.
