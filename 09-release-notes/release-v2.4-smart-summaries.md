# Release Notes - Smart Summaries v2.4

**Released:** November 2025
**Pillar:** AI Intelligence
**Status:** GA (generally available)

---

## What Shipped

Smart Summaries v2.4 is live for all Pro and Team users. It makes summaries shorter and more useful.

---

## Why We Built It

Summaries too long was the #2 complaint in user feedback - 14 mentions in the Q1 2026 NPS survey.

Users said: "I want bullet points, not an essay. Give me the 3 things that matter."

The previous model averaged 350 words per 30-minute meeting. That is too much to read quickly.

---

## What Changed for You

- Summaries are now 30% shorter on average.
- Key decisions are highlighted at the top - not buried in paragraphs.
- The summary structure is consistent: decisions first, then discussion points, then context.

---

## Impact

NPS improved from 22 (Q3 2025) to 34 (Q1 2026). That is a 12-point gain.

The "summaries too long" complaint dropped from the #2 theme to background noise. Promoters now cite summaries as a reason they recommend MeetFlow.

---

## Known Limitations

- Summaries are still 45-90 seconds to generate after a meeting ends. This is a known gap.
- Accuracy drops in noisy environments (coffee shops, open offices, poor mic quality). Transcription accuracy falls below 80% in those conditions, which affects summary quality.
- Speaker diarization (who said what) fails for meetings with 8+ attendees. Summaries may misattribute quotes.
