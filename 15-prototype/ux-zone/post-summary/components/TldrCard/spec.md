# TldrCard

The headline summary card at the top of /summary. Sparkle icon + "Quick recap" title + metric badges (duration / decisions / actions) on the right, paragraph body.

## Anatomy
- Card with header + body
- Header: sparkle icon + "Quick recap" title (left), 3 badges (right)
- Body: 3-4 sentence paragraph
