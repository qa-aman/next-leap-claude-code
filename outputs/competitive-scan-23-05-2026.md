### 1. Otter.ai

**Positioning:** Otter has shifted from "transcription tool" to "Conversational Knowledge Engine." The homepage hero reads: "Your AI notetaker is now also your Conversational Knowledge Engine." The message is about AI agents acting on meeting context, not just capturing it. Source: [otter.ai](https://otter.ai)

**Pricing tiers** (monthly billing, per user):

| Tier | Monthly | Annual |
|---|---|---|
| Basic | Free | Free |
| Pro | $16.99 | $8.33 |
| Business | $30.00 | $20.00 |
| Enterprise | Custom | Custom |

Source: [otter.ai/pricing](https://otter.ai/pricing)

**Free plan limits:**
- 300 monthly transcription minutes
- 3 lifetime audio/video file imports
- 5 maximum workspace members
- 25 conversations in history
- 20 AI Chat queries per user per month
- 5 AI meeting templates per user

Source: [otter.ai/pricing](https://otter.ai/pricing)

**Top 3 homepage differentiators:**
1. "Conversational Knowledge Engine" - AI chat searches across meetings AND connected apps (Gmail, Google Drive, Notion, Jira integration noted as "Coming Soon"). Positions Otter as a knowledge hub, not just a recorder.
2. AI Agents (SDR Agent, Recruiting Agent) - task-specific bots that automate post-meeting workflows. SDR Agent handles lead qualification and live demos; Recruiting Agent syncs candidate notes to Greenhouse.
3. OtterPilot - meeting assistant for Zoom, Teams, and Google Meet with bot-free desktop option. CRM push to Salesforce highlighted for sales users.

Source: [otter.ai](https://otter.ai)

**Product launches in last 90 days (window: 17-12-2025 to 17-03-2026):**
Otter's official blog does not provide publication dates for individual posts. The following features are labeled "NEW" on official pages but no official date in the 90-day window is confirmed: Otter MCP Server, Advanced meeting templates, AI Chat Connectors (Google Drive, Notion, Jira). Claude MCP integration and SCIM directory sync (for enterprise) are documented but undated on official pages.

**No dated launch confirmed in the 90-day window from official sources.** Source: [otter.ai/blog](https://otter.ai/blog)

### 2. Fireflies.ai

**Positioning:** "The #1 AI Assistant For Your Meetings." Leads with 95% transcription accuracy and 100+ language support. Emphasizes conversation intelligence (talk-time analytics, sentiment, topic tracking) as a differentiator for sales and CS teams. Source: [fireflies.ai](https://fireflies.ai)

**Pricing tiers** (monthly billing, per user):

| Tier | Monthly | Annual |
|---|---|---|
| Free | $0 | $0 |
| Pro | $18 | $10 |
| Business | $29 | $19 |
| Enterprise | $39 | $39 |

Source: [fireflies.ai/pricing](https://fireflies.ai/pricing)

**Free plan limits:**
- 400 minutes total storage (team, not per user)
- Unlimited transcription
- AI summaries: limited (not specified further on pricing page)
- 2-hour maximum recording per session
- AI Credits: not publicly disclosed per-tier breakdown

Source: [fireflies.ai/pricing](https://fireflies.ai/pricing)

**Top 3 homepage differentiators:**
1. Live Assist - real-time in-meeting panel delivering answers from the knowledge base, past decisions, and pricing during live calls. Targeted at sales, CS, and recruiting. Prominent "NEW" badge on homepage.
2. Conversation Intelligence - speaker talk-time, sentiment analysis, topic tracking. Positioned as a coaching and QA tool for revenue teams. Source: [fireflies.ai/conversation-intelligence](https://fireflies.ai/conversation-intelligence)
3. Fireflies MCP Server - connect meeting notes into Claude, ChatGPT, and other AI tools. Also labeled "NEW" on homepage.

Source: [fireflies.ai](https://fireflies.ai)

**Product launches in last 90 days (window: 17-12-2025 to 17-03-2026):**
Live Assist and Desktop App both launched **13-11-2025** - just outside the 90-day window. No official changelog or dated product release page is publicly accessible. The blog does not surface dated product launch posts for the target window from official sources.

**No dated launch confirmed in the 90-day window from official sources.** Source: [fireflies.ai/blog/fireflies-launches-live-assist-and-desktop-app/](https://fireflies.ai/blog/fireflies-launches-live-assist-and-desktop-app/)

### 3. Granola

**Positioning:** "The AI notepad for people in back-to-back meetings." No bot joins the call - Granola captures via computer audio, making it private by default and universal across all platforms (Zoom, Google Meet, Teams, Webex, Slack). Strong word-of-mouth among founders and PMs. Source: [granola.ai](https://www.granola.ai)

**Pricing tiers** (monthly billing, per user):

| Tier | Monthly | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Basic | $0 | Free tier |
| Business | $14 | Individuals and small teams |
| Enterprise | $35 | Governance controls, SSO, SCIM |

Source: [granola.ai/pricing](https://www.granola.ai/pricing)

**Free plan limits:**
- Unlimited meetings (no cap on count or duration publicly disclosed)
- Limited meeting history (retention limit not specified on pricing page)
- AI notes and AI Chat included
- Shared folders and custom templates included
- Multi-language support included
- Specific AI credit or session cap: not publicly disclosed

Source: [granola.ai/pricing](https://www.granola.ai/pricing)

**Top 3 homepage differentiators:**
1. No meeting bot - Granola captures via system audio without sending a bot into the call. Users control recording themselves, keeping it private and avoiding bot-rejection by participants.
2. Works on every platform - Zoom, Google Meet, Teams, Webex, Slack calls. No per-platform setup required.
3. Post-meeting AI actions - follow-up email drafting, action item extraction, budget extraction, one-click sharing to Slack, email, CRM, Notion.

Source: [granola.ai](https://www.granola.ai)

**Product launches in last 90 days (window: 17-12-2025 to 17-03-2026):**

| Date | Launch | Description |
|---|---|---|
| 04-02-2026 | Granola MCP | Connects meeting notes to Claude, ChatGPT, Cursor via Model Context Protocol |
| 02-02-2026 | Brand Redesign | New logo, typefaces, and visual system |
| 16-01-2026 | QoL improvements | Better Slack copy-paste, improved sign-in errors, refreshed calendar permissions |
| 15-01-2026 | Microsoft Sign-In | Sign in with Microsoft - supports Teams and Outlook authentication |
| 08-01-2026 | Smart regeneration | Smarter regeneration warnings, attendee cards on mentions |

Note: Series C ($125M, 25-03-2026) and Spaces/API launch fall just outside the window (8 days after the 17-03-2026 anchor). Included for awareness as they are the most significant strategic signal.

Source: [granola.ai/updates](https://www.granola.ai/updates)

### 4. Notion AI

**Positioning:** "Perfect meeting memory" - the pitch is that meeting notes live where work already happens. Notion frames AI Meeting Notes as a seamless extension of the workspace, not a standalone tool. Enterprise security is a top-3 message (SOC 2 Type 2, contractual zero data retention, granular permissions). Source: [notion.com/product/ai-meeting-notes](https://www.notion.com/product/ai-meeting-notes)

**Pricing tiers** (monthly billing, per user):

| Tier | Monthly | AI Meeting Notes? |
|---|---|---|
| Free | $0 | Trial only (limited) |
| Plus | $10 | Trial only (limited) |
| Business | $20 | Yes, full access |
| Enterprise | Custom | Yes, full access |

AI Meeting Notes is **only fully available on Business ($20/user/mo) and Enterprise tiers.** It is not bundled into the $10 Plus plan. Source: [notion.com/pricing](https://www.notion.com/pricing) and [notion.com/help/ai-meeting-notes](https://www.notion.com/help/ai-meeting-notes)

**Free plan limits:**
- 5MB max per file upload
- Limited collaborative blocks for multi-member workspaces
- 7-day page history
- 10 external guests max
- AI: trial access only (limited queries, no AI Meeting Notes)

Source: [notion.com/pricing](https://www.notion.com/pricing)

**Top 3 homepage differentiators for AI Meeting Notes:**
1. Native workflow integration - meeting notes connect directly to Notion tasks, projects, and databases. No context switching between a meeting tool and a work system.
2. Meeting-type summaries - AI tailors summaries to 1:1s, team syncs, client calls, interviews, and brainstorms automatically.
3. Enterprise-grade security - contractual protections against LLM training use, configurable transcript retention, SOC 2 Type 2. Positioned as a compliance-safe choice for enterprises.

Source: [notion.com/product/ai-meeting-notes](https://www.notion.com/product/ai-meeting-notes)

**Product launches in last 90 days (window: 17-12-2025 to 17-03-2026):**
Notion's official release changelog ([notion.com/releases](https://www.notion.com/releases)) does not surface any AI Meeting Notes-specific releases within this window. The earliest release shown is 17-04-2026. Monthly release pages for January-March 2026 returned HTTP 504 timeout errors and could not be confirmed.

**No AI Meeting Notes launch confirmed in the 90-day window from official sources.**
